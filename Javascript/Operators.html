Operators

Arithmetic

  • "+" a = 2 + 4
  • "-" a = 3 - 2
  • "*" a = 4 * 3
  • "/" a = 4 / 2
  • "++" a = a + 1
  • "--" a = a - 1

The modulus operator is used to get the remainder.

Assignment

  • "let" a = 0
  • "+=" a + = 4
  • "-=" a - = 4
  • "*=" a * = 4
  • "/=" a / = 4

Comparison

  • "==" Equal to
  • "!=" Not equal to
  • ">" Greater than
  • "<" Less than
  • ">=" Greater than or equal to
  • "<=" Less than or equal to

Using all operators in one function. In this example, players have been assigned names and today's score. Arithmetic allows for a combination of their total scores and comparison evaluates who has done better today AND overall.
